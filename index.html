About Me

I am an AI R&D Engineer with over 8 years of professional experience spanning Document Intelligence, Payment Automation, and Digital Transformation. My work sits at the intersection of Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Generative AI, and Vision-Language Models, backed by hands-on cloud expertise on AWS and GCP.

Currently based in Tokyo, Japan, I lead GenAI, Agentic AI, and Foundation Model solutions for enterprise clients across healthcare, telecommunications, and insurance domains.

I hold a Master of Engineering in Data Science & AI from the Asian Institute of Technology (Thailand) and a B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering from Begum Rokeya University (Bangladesh). I am also an open-source contributor to the Bangla Treebank (Universal Dependencies).
